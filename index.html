About Feni

Feni is a district located in southeastern Bangladesh. It's known for its culture, friendly people, and rich history. It's a peaceful town with a growing economy.Feni is a small but important district located in the southeastern part of Bangladesh. It is part of the Chattogram Division and is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and friendly community. Feni is well-connected by roads and has grown in importance due to trade, education, and agriculture. The Mohipal Flyover, the first four-lane flyover outside Dhaka, is one of its modern highlights. Despite its growth, Feni still maintains a peaceful and traditional lifestyle, making it a special place for both residents and visitors.
